Elevator Pitches

  1. For game masters who have a hard time coming up with encounters on the spot, the Monster Masher is a website that generates generic NPCs and combat descriptions that can be flavored to meet whatever the GM needs. Unlike using a predetermined random list of combat encounters, our product is designed so that the output is vague enough to be system agnostic while remaining detailed enough to stop game masters from pausing everything.
  2. For homeowners who are trying to maximize their time when washing multiple loads of laundry, the Laundry Lord is a website and app that dynamically keeps track of all the loads of laundry you plan to do in a certain amount of time, alerting you when it’s time for the next load. Unlike the Rinset app, our product doesn’t require anything other than the website or app to function.
  3. For whittlers who forget to sharpen their tools, the Sharpness Shrink is a website and app that tells the user when they should sharpen their tools after a certain amount of time. Unlike a traditional timer, our product allows whittlers to create individual profiles for each of their tools and track information such as time used overall, how long it can be used before needing to be sharpened, when it needs to completely replaced, and much more.
